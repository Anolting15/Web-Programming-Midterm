About the club

Our goals at the club are to facilitate interaction between students who are interested in the field of Computer Science. We will promote communication between students and faculty members in the Computer Science/Cyber Security departments. Also, we strive to provide opportunity to communicate with employers who are interested in students with Computer Science interest.

Where to meet

We meet every second and third Tuesday of the month. Location of meet is Newton Oaks Commons, room 210.
